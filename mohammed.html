About the University

Est. 1987

Tikrit University has demonstrated remarkable achievements across diverse disciplines since its establishment in Saladin Governorate.

Tikrit University is one of the rapidly developing educational institutions in Saladin Governorate. It encompasses a variety of scientific and humanities colleges, along with research centers and consulting offices. The university plays a vital role in community development by stimulating social, economic, and cultural progress.

University Achievements

Originally founded with a single college, the university has expanded to 22 colleges, two research centers, and two service centers for computing and continuing education.

Community Service

Tikrit University actively serves the community through collaborative research with governmental institutions, consultancy offices, clinics, and parallel education programs.
